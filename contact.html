Get in touch with Helsinki Harps

Whether you are interested in joining us, supporting the club, or just want to ask a question, we would be glad to hear from you.

Our club updates, discussions and changes to training schedules are communicated via our internal WhatsApp chat. We can add you when you come to training, or write to us.
