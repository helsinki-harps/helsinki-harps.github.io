Introduction

What are Gaelic Sports?

Gaelic Football and Hurling are the two most popular sports in the island of Ireland. They find their roots in the ancient games played in Ireland since ancient times. Their play is regulated by the Gaelic Athletics Association.

Who we are

Finland's oldest GAA club

Helsinki Harps has a long-standing place in Gaelic games in Finland, built on continuity, community, and a commitment to keeping the sport active and visible.

TRAINING
